Welcome to the First Western Balkan Conference in Mathematics and Applications-FWBCMA, June 10-12 2021.

The journal publishes original papers in English, in all fields of mathematical analysis. Usually 1 volume, with 4 issues per year.

Call For papers

Authors are invited to submit articles for publication. The manuscript must be original and written in English, and can be submitted to any member of the editorial board and should send to the secretariat of the Journal in this email:journalma@gmail.com

The Journal is indexed in Zentralblatt and will be indexed in Mathematical Reviews, MathSciNet
